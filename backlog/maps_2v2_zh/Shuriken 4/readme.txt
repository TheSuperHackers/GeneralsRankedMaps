Shuriken 4 is a FFA map that wants you to fight for your recources. You begin with
5 small supplypiles, 1 derrick and 1 reinforcementspad. The only 4 existing supplydocks
are placed squarewise in the middle, so it is reachable for everyone. Get one or two to
higher your chance of survive!
